Example searches

Operator '+' (this term must be present) and '-' (this term must not be present) other terms are optional.
Boolean operators AND, OR and NOT (also written &&, || and !) are also supported.
Wildcard searches can use ? to replace a single character (PTP?22), and * to replace zero or more characters.
  • ALL: +john +todd +t1d
  • ALL: +PTPN22 +IL* +todd
  • ALL: +title:(IL2 OR PTPN22) +"Nat. Genet."
  • ALL: rs2476601 (filter on marker id and journal title)
  • ALL: abstract:"development of type 1 diabetes in NOD mice"
  • GENE: phosphatase -protein_coding
  • GENE: dbxrefs.swissprot:Q9Y2R2
  • GENE: description:"protein tyrosine phosphatase" -protein_coding
  • PUBLICATION: +Onengut-Gumuscu +"Nat. Genet."
  • PUBLICATION: +Onengut-Gumuscu -"Nat. Genet."
  • PUBLICATION: +IL2 +diabetes +Wicker
